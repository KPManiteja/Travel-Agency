Who We Are

Founded in 2015, 7HillsHolidays began as a small team of passionate travelers with a simple mission: to create personalized, authentic travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist routes.

Today, we've grown into a leading travel agency with offices in 3 countries, but we've kept our personal touch. Each itinerary is still handcrafted by our team of destination experts who have actually lived in or extensively traveled to the places they recommend.

Rajesh Kumar, Founder

Our Mission

We believe travel should be transformative, not transactional. Our mission is to connect travelers with the essence of each destination through carefully curated experiences that respect local cultures and environments.
