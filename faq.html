Booking Questions

You can book directly through our website by clicking "Book Now" on any tour page, or contact our travel consultants via phone or email for personalized assistance.

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and bank transfers. For international bookings, we also accept PayPal.

Payment & Cancellation

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a 90% refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund but we offer travel credits for future bookings. Some special tours may have different policies.

Yes! Most tours can be booked with a 25% deposit, with the balance due 30 days before departure. We also offer EMI options through select banking partners.

Travel Preparation

You'll need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visas (we'll guide you), travel insurance, vaccination certificates if required, and printed copies of your itinerary and hotel vouchers.

Absolutely! We provide complete visa assistance including document checklist, application filling, appointment scheduling, and follow-up. Some visas may require personal appearance.

During Your Trip

We provide 24/7 emergency support during your trip. You'll receive local contact numbers and our team will be available to assist with any issues from missed connections to medical emergencies.

Yes, our local guides can help modify your itinerary based on your preferences and local conditions. Some changes may incur additional costs depending on the arrangements.
